Airfoil's Perfect Partner

Airfoil Satellite makes using Airfoil even better. Airfoil Satellite
lets you receive audio from Airfoil, and remotely control it as well.

Get More With Airfoil Satellite

Receive Audio

Just launch Airfoil Satellite, and your Mac will appear as an
audio output for Airfoil running on another Mac or PC. You can also send
audio to Airfoil Satellite directly from iTunes or iOS.

Remotely Control Airfoil

Airfoil Satellite can also remotely control all the main functionality of Airfoil.
Toggle outputs, adjust volumes, and even change sources.

Other Platforms, Too

Airfoil Satellite is also available on other platforms, including Windows,
iOS, and Android, so you can receive audio and control Airfoil from all
your devices. Visit our site for more details.
